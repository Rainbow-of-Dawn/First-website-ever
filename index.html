Our Heroes' background

Captain Garnet was born on a ship in the sea of Marmara.

Her father, the infamous pirate Captain Cutlass,
trained her to become an elite strategist, trader and fighter.

She quickly became the Quartermaster of her father's ship,
and earned the name Garnet because of her red hair.

They sailed off the Mediterranean Sea together, even till Egypt.

During a cannon attack ambush near the Nile,
their ship got boarded by enemies.

An infiltrated spy of King Aeetes shielded her from harm,
but Captain Cutlass got fatally wounded by the cannons.

At her father's death, the crew designated her to be the new Captain.

Since then, she and King Aeetes's infiltrated spy became lovers.

Captain Garnet's network and reputation grew even overseas.

Renowned for being fearless and respected by many,
she can defeat and pillage several ships per day.

Thankful towards King Aeetes for having saved her life,
she has decided to dedicate her life to serving the interests of Colchis.

With dozens of ships under her command, she is the pair of eyes,
ears and hands of the Kingdom over the Black Sea.

Raised as a Knight of Colchis, Lord Ebony came from a wealthy family.

He is a specialist of nobility, negotiation and war.

Lord Ebony knows every noble family of the country,
which gives him a huge political influence and power.

Agile and clever, he is the finest strategist of the Kingdom,
hence why he coordinates the Colchis Army with our General.

His loyalty to King Aeetes is without equal.

Lord Ebony's fighting skills are just as excellent, above all with swords.

His father owns a castle in the North East forest,
this is where he met Lady Chartreuse.

The two became engaged, and King Aeetes supports their union,
which helps fighting off social class discrimination.

Lord Ebony and his allies helped the Kingdom win the battle of Cape Jason,
South West of Colchis, where his courage and skills came to light.

He regularly trains young recruits in our Army, and he takes care of the elder,
particularly our war veterans, by funding care programs for their cause.

Everyday, Lord Ebony fights for Colchis, whether it be with good deeds,
like giving to local charities, or by leading sentinel and patrol operations.

Lady Chartreuse's ancestry fled from war and sought refuge in Colchis.

She grew up in poverty. Her family raised her in a small hut,
in the heart of the North East forest.

Everything they could get mattered. They hunted their own food.

She was also taught how to use plants as food, medicine and poison.

One day during a hunt, Lady Chartreuse met Lord Ebony,
they became inseparable since then.

None in the Kingdom of Colchis is better with bows and arrows than her.

During the lastest Olympic Games of Greece in Olympus,
she was granted the gold medal of archery.

Master hunter and survival expert, she is a precious ally to Colchis.

She has become the National Archery Champion of the Kingdom.

This is how she earned her title of "Lady of the Kingdom".

The opening of the Faculty of Medicine was celebrated with her presence,
notably by making a register of all medicinal plants of the Kingdom.

She also brought her contribution to the Colchis School of Archery,
where she teaches as an Emerit Profesor.
